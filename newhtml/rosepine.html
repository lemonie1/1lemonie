Unlemonie's Gone iNsAnE!
ITS FINALLY OVER AND ITS STILL SO FAR FROM FINISHED // TOOK ME 3 WHOLE DAYS // I LIKE UNDERTALES // I DON'T WANNA DO NEOCITIES MAN // LIVING SINCE 18TH OCTOBER!

I might've made at least 20 different websites, and practically used none of them. All of them are beyond defunct. That is, until I was going through Neocities and found this layout. Sweet, short, simple.

Now I give you absolutely no guarantee that I'm ever going to continue using this.

Okay, so here's my introduction: I am an idiot + I like a shitton of things that keep changing. I like to call them arcs, because of the manga-sense of an arc and because it usually follows a shape like that, slow build up, climax, slow end. My arcs usually last a few weeks to a couple months, depending on how much I like the subject. Sometimes they repeat each other, like how I liked robotics, then stopped, then liked it again. Who knows why.

It's very hard to summarize myself in a few words but I'm an enjoyer of very niche stuff that looks coool and stuff. My 'core' interests are: preserving stuff, applying math to things I like (like Mario physics, Rubik's cubes, robotics etc), drawing, worldbuilding, video games, programming and a tiny bit of biology (I dig evolution, genetics and neuroscience). I would like to get into football but I'm the least athletic person I know. I REALLY like old stuff liked vintage cameras, Frutiger Aero and VHS tapes.
