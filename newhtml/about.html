Unlemonie's Gone iNsAnE!
ITS FINALLY OVER // AND YET MOST OF IT IS STILL W.I.P // I LIKE UNDERTALES // I DON'T WANNA DO NEOCITIES MAN

I might've made at least 20 different websites, and practically used none of them. All of them are beyond defunct. That is, until I was going through Neocities and found this layout. Sweet, short, simple.

Now I give you absolutely no guarantee that I'm ever going to continue using this.

Here's the tragic backstory for this site. Short story short, I got insanely bored, I have nothing to do or no friends in my apartment so I simply decided to make another website out of the three trillion I already have so that I can talk about Inception or lucid dreaming or Mario or digital camera sensors or whatever the hell I'm on about at the moment.

It pretty much started from my constant urge to deposit my highly interest-changing novelty seeking brain, because there's a limit that my friends can entertain. Beyond that they're just gonna ignore me, which to be honest is fine, because I could talk about one thing that I've liked for one day forever. It's not easy to 'remove' an interest from my mind. So I thought, hey, why not make a website where I can just dump all my thoughts and feelings? So here we are! The millionth iteration of a site for my cognitive vomit!
